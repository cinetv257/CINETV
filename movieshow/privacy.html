2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial data (for free users), or precise location data without your explicit consent.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain our streaming service

  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform

  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide assistance

  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Our free service is supported by third-party advertisements. These ads help us keep the platform free. Ad providers may use cookies and similar technologies to deliver relevant ads.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information from our records.
